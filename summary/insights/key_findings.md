# Key Insights

## 1. Indirect Flooding Risk
Even inland villages are affected due to groundwater rise and drainage inefficiency.

## 2. Environmental Impact
Flooding leads to:
- Soil degradation
- Salinization
- Reduced agricultural productivity

## 3. Climate Drivers
- Sea level rise
- Land subsidence
- Reduced sediment supply

## 4. Risk Pattern
Flooding is often slow and chronic, not sudden.

## 5. Strategic Insight
Flood risk assessment should include inland areas, not only coastal zones.
